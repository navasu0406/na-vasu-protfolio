About


I am a music producer, singer, songwriter, and aspiring filmmaker.


I've been producing music for over 6 years now, and it's become a huge part of who I am. From building songs from the ground up to mixing tracks and designing sounds, I love every step of the process.


I'm always chasing that perfect melody & sound that connects.


Over the years, I've made a bunch of independent songs and also composed background scores for short films and live plays.
